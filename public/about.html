About


What Problem are We Trying to Solve?

There is an abundance of job announcements/offers online, but it can be overwhelming. Some job advertisements do not provide necessary information like salary in their descriptions while others provide too much information. Either way, this leaves a job-seeking person unprepared and unmotivated to apply to jobs - making their job search last longer.


Our Stakeholders

The people who care about this problem are people who may be unemployed, or are simply trying to look for a new job. Our stakeholders are mostly the public, and not a specific company or organization.


The Solution

Currently, there are many different websites that allow companies to post job announcements. Some of these websites are difficult to navigate and provide unnecessary and/or little information about job openings. Our solution will ease the process of finding a job. We have done this by providing important information to our users like salary, location, and job type.
